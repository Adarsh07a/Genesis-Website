Our Story

About Genesis Nature Science

"Nature's Science, Farmer's Pride"

Growing with India's Farmers

Genesis Nature Science Pvt. Ltd. is a dedicated agricultural company headquartered in Sirpur, Telangana. We are committed to delivering high-quality hybrid seeds, advanced crop nutrition solutions, and effective plant protection products to farmers across India.

Our research-driven approach ensures that every product we offer — from hybrid vegetables and field crops to micronutrients and bio-pesticides — is scientifically formulated for maximum yield, quality, and sustainability.

We believe in building long-term relationships with farming communities by providing reliable inputs, technical guidance, and innovative solutions that make modern agriculture accessible and profitable for every farmer.
